About Me

Building stuff brings me joy.

I am a Software Engineer and UI Designer with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science. During my studies, I found many areas I was passionate about so it was difficult to decide what path I should take. Ultimately, I narrowed down my choices to three: Artificial Intelligence (AI), Cybersecurity, and Project Management. Fortunately, these fields interconnect in significant ways.

To excel in security, it is necessary to understand the systems I aim to secure. Moreover, AI is crucial in enhancing software functionality and improving lives, warranting its protection and maintenance. Additionally, having led multiple projects during university, I aimed to bolster my leadership skills to ensure seamless team dynamics. My goal is to develop secure, groundbreaking applications that enrich and enhance lives.

Lately, I have been nurturing a passion for UI Design paying keen attention to learning and applying design principles, trends, and best practices such as accessibility.

I am seeking job opportunities where I can contribute, expand my knowledge, and continue to grow. If you have an opportunity that aligns with my skills and experience, I would love to explore it further. Feel free to reach out.
